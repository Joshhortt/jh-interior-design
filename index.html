Specification Phase

Workplace Specification: Our workplace consultants help you specify your needs for the workplace: the amount and type of space needed as well as the areas for different activities. An analysis of the results of the work environment survey and interviews will be created.

Design: Our experienced interior designers design a flexible workplace to fit your needs both now and in the future. The design is based on the space plan created during the specification phase on the basis of various needs.

Communicaton: Our implementation team delivers the turnkey solution as well as takes care of the recycling when needed. Our consultants help you with the change management.

And once implemented, our consultants help you continuously optimise and develop your workplace according to your changing needs.
